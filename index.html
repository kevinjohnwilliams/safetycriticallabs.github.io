What we do

A certification authority
for AI systems.

Safety Critical Labs conducts structured assessments of AI systems against the SCL AI Requirements Framework. A formal determination is issued at a defined classification level with documented findings on record.

Assessments are conducted against a published, citable standard. The framework is publicly available, versioned, and open to review. Anyone can read it, audit it, and challenge it.

A certificate states what was assessed, against which version of the framework, at which classification level, and what was found. It records that a system was formally evaluated against a defined standard with a reproducible methodology.

What certification produces

A formal record.
Issued on determination.

Each certificate records the system assessed, the framework version applied, the classification level, and the determination reached. The findings remain on record.

Documented findings
A complete assessment record
Every assessment produces a formal findings document covering all nine requirement areas at the applicable classification level.
Classification level
Consequence-based classification
Systems are classified by consequence of failure using a structured likelihood-versus-consequence methodology.
Framework version
Assessed against a published standard
Every determination references a specific framework version. The standard is publicly available and open to review by any party.
Surveillance requirement
Validity tied to configuration
Certification is valid while the assessed configuration is maintained. Annual surveillance confirms continued compliance.
Formal determination
Three outcomes, documented regardless
Certified, Conditionally Certified, or Not Certified. Each determination is documented and placed on record regardless of outcome.
Scope of assessment
Nine requirement areas assessed
Data partitioning, bias detection, test coverage, continuous validation, hallucination prevention, OOD detection, adversarial robustness, explainability, and human-AI teaming.
